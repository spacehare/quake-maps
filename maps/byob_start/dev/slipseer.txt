[h1]Bring Your Own Bottle[/h1]

[B]BYOB[/B] is a Quake jam where mappers were required to make their own textures.

Co-hosted by Wons and Rabbit, with assistance from Quiet and the Quake Mapping Discord staff. The active mapping period lasted from June 18th to July 3rd.
